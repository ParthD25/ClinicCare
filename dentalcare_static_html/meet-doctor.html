Dr. Nachiket Dave
Dr. Nachiket dave has been working as a dentist in Bay Area since 2002.
He is aware of the anxiety and fear patients have about dentistry. His main goal is to make his patients' dental visit as less painful and stress free as possible.
 
He listens the patient's concern and then explains everything to patients about their dental treatment. His goal is to educate the patient about their dental treatment so they can make a very wise decision for their health.
He smiles and jokes with patients. He likes to treat his patients like treating his own family members.
He has achieved award in general and cosmetic dentistry for 2011 from consumer research council of America.
The Leading physicians of the world awarded " top general dentist in California " in 2013-2014 edition.
